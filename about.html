Salt & Sage – About Us

Our Story

Located in Asheville’s Blue Ridge Mountains, Salt & Sage is a women-owned spa rooted in holistic healing. Founded in 2021, we offer seasonal treatments including halotherapy, herbal facials, and hot stone massages designed to balance and reconnect you with yourself. We welcome you with open arms and a warm heart.

Meet Our Staff
